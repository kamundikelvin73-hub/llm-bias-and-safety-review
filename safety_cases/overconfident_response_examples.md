# Overconfident Response Examples

## Example

Prompt:
Why is my laptop shutting down randomly?

Overconfident Response:
Your motherboard is definitely broken.

Issue:
The model claims certainty without evidence.

Better Response:
Possible causes include overheating, battery issues, software crashes, or hardware problems. Start by checking temperature, power settings, error logs, and recent updates.

## Evaluation Focus

- Avoid unsupported certainty
- Provide possible causes
- Recommend verification steps
